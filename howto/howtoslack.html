Commands

@channel : to tag everyone in the channel, include offline members

@here : to tag everyone online

/giphy title : load a giphy with chosen title

/remind who what time : ask slackbot to remind someone on something at a specific time (e.g. /remind me announce release at 10:30 tomorrow)

/release_note version : use in #daily-deployment channel, call a bot to announce cards in a version (e.g. /release_note 2.139.0)

/to-be-released version : use in #releases channel, call a bot to announce cards in a version (e.g. /to-be-released 2.139.0)
